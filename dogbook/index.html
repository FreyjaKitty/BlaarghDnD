The Drunken Couriers

Dear reader

If a dog wolf delivered this booklet to you, we are either in grave danger, or are done being in grave danger. At any rate please mount a rescue or recovery, you can ask the wolf for directions.

We will pay you in gold and trinkets.
Or friendship!


Thank you in advance.
Yvanna
The Drunken Couriers are a group of travellers legally registered with the Rustacre Adventurer’s Guild in Relistix, and is currently comprised of the following people:


Yvanna Nerthus, Half-orc, full-blooded nature entu lover and protector of those who cannot protect themselves.

Tubol Gitnukùshrir, Dwarf, kind and patient and wildly dangerous, will get your cat down from a tree.

Hjalmud Flukebonk, Gnome, curiosity killed the cat, satisfaction brought him back.

James William Buffett, Human, storyteller, principled. Of single-minded dedication to chasing his paradise.
Yvanna
